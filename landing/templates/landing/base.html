Quick and easy web site planning from the earliest stage of development

We simplify your work at all stages. Your customers will enjoy participating in the development process, and they will see the beautiful and understandable structure of the future website

FlowMapp facilitates collaborative work on planning ux-strategy and design

With FlowMapp, colleagues and clients can work together to create a great sitemap in just minutes. You can invite anyone to the project at any stage to work together. Each participant can make comments, and each participant can also edit the structure and descriptions of the site pages

Simple and intuitive sitemap creation

Develop a structure, create web pages, and alter the order of the pages of your site. FlowMapp gives developers the ability to choose cover templates for each page, add page labels and switch the view of the sitemap. And, of course, you can use Drag and Drop page cards with friendly animations!

Add page description

Add pages descriptions, upload files with content or design and discuss these actions with your development team and your client in real time. You can plan and save the content of the future site in the FlowMapp service itself, until your website is ready for launch.
